Services

Our clinic offers a comprehensive range of dental services to cater to your oral health needs. From routine check-ups and cleanings to advanced procedures like dental implants and cosmetic dentistry, our experienced 1 team is committed to providing top-quality care. We prioritize patient comfort and utilize state-of-the-art technology to ensure a painless and efficient experience.

RCT

Root Canal Treatment (RCT) is a procedure designed to save a damaged tooth. When the tooth's pulp becomes infected, RCT removes the infected tissue and seals the root canal.

Dental Implant

Dental implants are a revolutionary solution for missing teeth. These titanium posts are surgically placed into your jaw bone

It acting as artificial tooth roots. Once integrated, they provide a strong foundation for crowns, bridges, or dentures.

Dental Crown

A dental crown is a tooth-shaped cap that is placed over a damaged tooth.

Crowns can be used to protect a weak tooth from breaking or to hold together parts of a cracked tootwith a large filling.  

Dental Filling

It is a common and effective way to repair tooth decay. Our dentists carefully remove the decayed portion of your tooth and fill the cavity with a durable material
